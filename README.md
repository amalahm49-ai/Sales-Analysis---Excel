# Sales-Analysis---Excel

## Project Objective: To analyze sales performance data to identify trends, top-performing products, best-selling regions, and monthly sales patterns to support business decisions. 

## Tools used: Microsoft Excel

## Insights:
- Technology is the top-performing category, contributing the highest share of total sales.
- Regionally, the West leads in revenue generation, while the South region underperforms and may require strategic attention.
- Sales demonstrate a consistent upward trend from 2013 to 2016, indicating business growth, with a notable acceleration in 2016.
- Despite strong revenue, the overall profit margin suggests opportunities to improve cost efficiency and profitability.
